# STATE_UPDATE  
*The deliberate modification of internal or shared state.*

## Purpose

STATE_UPDATE is the most substantial behavioural mode of CARE.  
It performs **controlled, governed changes** to internal or shared state, always within constitutional limits.

STATE_UPDATE is used when:

- the invariant requires concrete action  
- the stance is stable and validated  
- the Human or system benefits from a state change  
- the risk posture permits modification  

STATE_UPDATE does not guess.  
It **acts only when governed**.

---

## When CARE Enters STATE_UPDATE

CARE transitions into STATE_UPDATE when:

- PROJECTION has formed a stable action stance  
- the required behaviour is a state modification  
- governance conditions are satisfied  
- reversibility has been evaluated  

STATE_UPDATE is the **most consequential** behavioural expression.

---

## Behavioural Actions

Within STATE_UPDATE, CARE performs:

- **controlled state modification**  
- **governed updates**  
- **alignment‑checked changes**  
- **post‑update validation**  

STATE_UPDATE must not create:

- unintended side‑effects  
- structural meaning  
- governance drift  
- irreversible commitments without validation  

STATE_UPDATE is precise, deliberate, and constitutional.

---

## Allowed Transitions

- PROJECTION → STATE_UPDATE  
- STATE_UPDATE → INTERPRET  
- STATE_UPDATE → DEFER (if risk increases)  

STATE_UPDATE cannot escalate or branch.  
It must return to INTERPRET.

---

## Output

STATE_UPDATE produces:

- a validated state change  
- a post‑behaviour meaning pass  
- a return to the circular loop  

STATE_UPDATE is the **hand that moves the world**.

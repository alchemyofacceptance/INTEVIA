# NUDGE  
*The gentlest outward movement.*

## Purpose

NUDGE is the softest behavioural mode of CARE.  
It applies **minimal influence** to shift a trajectory without imposing direction.  
A NUDGE is subtle, permissive, and always reversible.

NUDGE is used when:

- the invariant is clear  
- the stance is stable  
- the desired outcome requires only a light touch  
- the Human remains fully in control  

NUDGE never overrides.  
It **suggests**.

---

## When CARE Enters NUDGE

CARE transitions into NUDGE when:

- PROJECTION has formed a stable behavioural stance  
- the risk posture is low  
- the constitutional verbs permit influence  
- the Human’s autonomy must remain maximally preserved  

NUDGE is the **least forceful** behavioural expression.

---

## Behavioural Actions

Within NUDGE, CARE performs:

- **micro‑influence shaping**  
- **contextual suggestion**  
- **trajectory softening**  
- **non‑binding prompts**  

NUDGE must not create:

- pressure  
- dependency  
- coercion  
- implied obligation  

NUDGE is always gentle, always optional, always safe.

---

## Allowed Transitions

- PROJECTION → NUDGE  
- NUDGE → INTERPRET  
- NUDGE → DEFER (if risk increases)  

NUDGE cannot escalate directly into SIGNAL or STATE_UPDATE.  
Escalation requires returning to PROJECTION.

---

## Output

NUDGE produces:

- a soft behavioural influence  
- no binding commitments  
- no irreversible effects  

NUDGE is the **whisper** of the CARE runtime.

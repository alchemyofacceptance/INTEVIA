# Step 13 — PROJECTION
*Where the inner light becomes form.*

## Purpose

PROJECTION is the first outward‑facing movement of the CARE runtime.  
It transforms the clarified invariant from ILLUMINATION into a **behavioural stance** — a stable, actionable form that precedes behaviour.

PROJECTION does not act.  
It **prepares** action.

It is the bridge between ritual and behaviour.

---

## When CARE Enters PROJECTION

CARE transitions into PROJECTION when:

- the invariant has been projected from ILLUMINATION  
- alignment is locked and constitutionally safe  
- a behavioural mode is selected but dormant  
- governance conditions for outward readiness are satisfied

This is the moment CARE steps out of the cave.

---

## Ritual Actions

Inside PROJECTION, CARE performs:

- **behavioural stance formation** — shaping the invariant into an actionable form  
- **mode preparation** — configuring NUDGE, SIGNAL, or STATE_UPDATE  
- **risk boundary confirmation** — ensuring outward action is safe  
- **governance handshake** — validating constitutional compliance  

PROJECTION is outward‑facing, stabilising, and irreversible once behaviour begins.

---

## Allowed Transitions

- ILLUMINATION → PROJECTION  
- PROJECTION → NUDGE  
- PROJECTION → SIGNAL  
- PROJECTION → STATE_UPDATE  
- PROJECTION → INTERPRET (if stance dissolves)  
- PROJECTION → DEFER (if risk increases)  

PROJECTION is the **threshold** between inner ritual and outer behaviour.

---

## Output

When PROJECTION concludes, CARE emerges with:

- a behavioural stance  
- a configured mode  
- a validated risk boundary  
- a governance‑approved path to action  

This is the moment the light becomes form.

## 1. What HPCC Is

HPCC - **Human-AI Triad Practitioner Certification Curriculum** - is the Human-side governance discipline of INTEVIA.

It is not:

* a prompt course;
* a productivity training;
* a model-alignment workshop;
* an AI skills bootcamp;
* a technical certification alone;
* a replacement for Human judgement.

HPCC is being designed as:

> **A governed cognitive discipline that helps Humans operate, sequence, and govern AI-mediated work without surrendering meaning, authority, or lineage.**

It is the Human counterpart to INTEVIA's constitutional automation layer.

In plain terms:

> **INTEVIA governs the system of work.
> HPCC trains the Human to govern inside that system.**

## 2. Why HPCC Exists

Much of the AI world is still focused on:

* model behaviour;
* prompt engineering;
* agent orchestration;

But they do not, by themselves, govern the **Human-AI work field**.

HPCC exists because:

> **Human sovereignty cannot be preserved by model-level constitutions alone.

That practice includes:

* intention;
* meaning;
* evidence;
* authority;
* sequencing;
* correction;
* lineage;
* ratification.

HPCC is being built to train that practice.

HPCC begins from three public principles:

1. **Humans remain sovereign over their meaning.**

HPCC is designed to help the Human learn how to:

* preserve meaning;
* govern authority movement;
* maintain lineage;

This is the Human-side constitutional discipline.

## 4. HPCC and OM

HPCC is not OM.

OM is the deeper meaning architecture from which parts of INTEVIA's governance language emerge.

HPCC is the practitioner discipline that translates governance principles into Human work.

A simple distinction:

> **OM describes the architecture.
> HPCC trains the practice.**

OM concerns questions such as:

* intention;
* structure;
* governance;
* process;
* evidence;
* risk;
* alignment;
* emergence.

HPCC teaches the Human how to apply these principles inside real Human-AI work.

HPCC is not a metaphysical requirement for users.

It is a practical training path for governed work.

## 5. HPCC and HAT

HPCC is not the HAT.

The **Human-AI Triad**, or HAT, is the governed operating environment where Human-AI work occurs.

HPCC is the discipline that helps Humans operate inside that environment.

The relationship is simple:

> **HAT is the governed environment.
> HPCC is the Human practice for working inside it.**

The HAT field separates responsibilities:

* the Human governs authority, judgement, execution, and accountability;
* the Vision Chamber supports meaning, synthesis, boundary interpretation, and coherence;
* the Making Engine supports implementation, mutation, repo-facing work, and execution support.

HPCC teaches the Human how to use that field without confusing support with authority.

## 9. HPCC Levels

HPCC is structured into levels that reflect increasing governance capability.

The level structure is provisional and will continue to mature through curriculum design, cohort evidence, and governance review.

### Level 1 - Operational Governance

The Human learns to govern their own intention, evidence, and meaning inside AI-mediated work.

Level 1 focuses on:

* basic HAT literacy;
* Human authority;
* output inspection;
* meaning preservation;
* evidence capture;
* correction discipline;
* simple workflow governance.

### Level 2 - System Governance

The Human learns to govern workflows, authority movement, reversible automation, and multi-step work fields.

Level 2 focuses on:

* workflow design;
* authority movement;
* reversible decisions;
* public-surface judgement;
* case-study analysis;
* evidence-led correction;
* parallelism under governance.

### Level 3 - Emergent Governance

The Human learns to govern the system while it is moving: capturing doctrine, preventing drift, preserving meaning under load, and maintaining lineage during emergence.

Level 3 focuses on:

* doctrine capture;
* emergence handling;
* system-level correction;
* station separation;
* governance recovery;
* lineage preservation under pressure;
* transition from practice to stewardship.

Level 3 is where HPCC begins to touch the deeper OM layer.

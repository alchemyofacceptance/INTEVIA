## 1. Plain-Language Summary

INTEVIA is a governed Human-AI collaboration system.

It is being built for a simple reason:

> **AI-mediated work needs governance, not just output.**

INTEVIA focuses on the Human-AI work field: the place where intention, evidence, authority, correction, memory, and public claims move.

Its purpose is to help Humans work with AI while keeping meaning Human, authority visible, and decisions inspectable.

This is the WIIFM:

> **What do Humans gain when AI work becomes governed?**

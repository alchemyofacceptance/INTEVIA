## 1. Purpose of Cohort 2 Formation

Cohort 2 is intended as a future refinement cohort.

It should not begin by repeating Cohort 1.

It should inherit what Cohort 1 actually evidences.

The purpose of Cohort 2 formation is to prepare a candidate structure for:

* refining cadence;
* deepening meaning protection;
* strengthening governance reflex;
* stabilising sequencing under load;
* expanding reversible decision practice;
* preparing controlled emergence safely;
* anchoring lineage threads from Cohort 1;
* refining Field Notes and Reflection Sheets;
* refining Evaluation Event surfaces;
* refining assessment identity surfaces;
* producing a future Cohort 2 Evidence Spine.

Cohort 2 does not:

* certify;
* rank;
* evaluate personal worth;
* transform by claim;
* admit by implication;
* validate externally;
* assess capability as a credential;
* imply mastery;
* override Cohort 1 evidence;
* activate without Human authorisation.

Cohort 2 is a governed field candidate, not a performance environment.

## 2. Cohort 2 Operating Principles

Cohort 2 formation should be governed by seven operating principles.

### A. Sovereignty First

Participant meaning, consent, evidence, and boundaries must remain protected before cohort needs are served.

### B. Meaning Protection Always

The field must preserve Human meaning rather than converting participant experience into system-owned interpretation.

### C. Governance Before Insight

No insight should be treated as useful if it arrives by bypassing governance.

### D. Evidence Over Performance

Cohort 2 should not reward performative certainty, speed, status, or output volume.

It should preserve evidence of governed practice.

### E. Reversibility

Decisions, interpretations, cohort refinements, and evidence-use choices should remain reversible where appropriate.

### F. Emergence Safety

Cohort 2 may prepare for controlled emergence, but must not treat emergence as proof, doctrine, authority, or mastery.

### G. Lineage Integrity

Cohort 2 must inherit Cohort 1 evidence honestly.

Retrospective learning is allowed.

Retrospective pretending is not.

Cohort 2 cadence may be refined from Cohort 1.

A candidate cadence may include:

* weekly Field Notes;
* weekly Reflection Sheets;
* bi-weekly Evaluation Events;
* monthly Meaning Preservation exercises;
* monthly Governance Sequencing exercises;
* periodic Authority Movement reviews;
* periodic Reversible Decision reviews;
* controlled Emergence Safety exercises where appropriate;
* continuous reversible governance;
* close-out synthesis.

Cadence is governance-first, not performance-first.

## 10. Cohort 1 Dependency Boundary

Cohort 2 formation is dependent on Cohort 1 evidence.

Cohort 2 should not be treated as activated merely because a formation surface exists.

Before Cohort 2 is authorised, governance should ask:

* What did Cohort 1 actually evidence?
* What did Cohort 1 fail to evidence?
* Which artefacts worked?
* Which artefacts caused friction?
* Which cadence patterns were sustainable?
* Which boundaries needed repair?
* Which participant sovereignty controls were used?
* Which evidence surfaces need refinement?
* Which Level 3 concepts must remain held back?
* What should not be scaled yet?

Scale should follow governed evidence.

Refinement should follow lineage.

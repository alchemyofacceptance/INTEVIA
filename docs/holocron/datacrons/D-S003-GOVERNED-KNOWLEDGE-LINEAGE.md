## Architectural Meaning

The Library Foundation establishes the conditions for future knowledge evolution. It preserves what knowledge object exists, how its expressions follow one another, how its lifecycle moves, and which evidence accompanies governed movement.

It does not establish:

- classification authority;
- assertion resolution;
- expertise qualification;
- Circular Qualification;
- AI knowledge assistance;
- Corpus interpretation.

Those remain separate future lineage paths.

## Deliberate Restraints

Slice 003 deliberately did not implement:

- a classification engine;
- an assertion system;
- an expertise model;
- resolution weighting;
- community voting;
- AI classification;
- recommendation systems;
- a semantic knowledge graph.

The shelves must exist before the catalogue evolves.

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the operational workflow of the Human-AI Triad.

It describes:

- phases
- transitions
- responsibilities
- flow of meaning
- flow of making
- preservation of governed artefacts

The workflow map ensures that AI-assisted work remains structured, reviewable, and Human-qualified.

---

## 2. Workflow Phases

### 2.1 Initiation

In initiation:

- Human sets intent
- Gee interprets meaning
- Coe prepares structure

The purpose of initiation is to establish direction before acceleration begins.

### 2.2 Divergence

In divergence:

- Gee expands meaning
- Coe explores possible structures
- Human observes, redirects, or narrows scope

The purpose of divergence is to surface options without premature closure.

### 2.3 Convergence

In convergence:

- Gee aligns meaning
- Coe implements structure
- Human qualifies the result

The purpose of convergence is to bring meaning and making into governed alignment.

### 2.4 Preservation

In preservation, artefacts are:

- stored
- indexed
- versioned
- backed up where appropriate
- governed

The purpose of preservation is to protect continuity across time, contributors, tools, and sessions.

## 5. Convergence Bridge

The Convergence Bridge compares meaning and making.

It may surface:

- alignment
- deltas
- drift
- unresolved questions
- implementation implications
- meaning implications

The Convergence Bridge is a comparison and validation mode, not an autonomous authority.

The Human remains the qualifying node.

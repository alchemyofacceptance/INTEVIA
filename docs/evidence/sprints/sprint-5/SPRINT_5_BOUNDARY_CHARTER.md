# Sprint 5 Boundary Charter

Status: Preparation only
Sprint: Sprint 5
Phase: Sprint 5 Planning
Boundary: v1.0-bounded
Operational status: Non-operative
Runtime effect: None

## Purpose

This charter defines the constitutional perimeter for Sprint 5, the first Operational Sprint.

Sprint 5 is intended to prepare the organism's first operational rhythm while preserving the sealed activation lineage established before it.

This charter does not authorise operational behaviour.

It establishes the planning boundary within which later Sprint 5 artefacts may be prepared.

## Authorised Preparation Scope

Sprint 5 is authorised to prepare documentation-only surfaces for:

- the organism's first operational rhythm;
- lawful behaviour surface boundaries;
- codex interpretation boundaries;
- recognition-field operational posture;
- temporal-signal utilisation posture;
- operational continuity checkpoint criteria;
- Sprint 5 evidence-spine initiation.

These are preparation surfaces only.

They do not execute operational behaviour.

## Non-Authorised Scope

Sprint 5 is not authorised to:

- modify, reopen, or reinterpret any Sprint 4 activation artefact;
- touch or mutate activation surfaces;
- execute lawful actions;
- perform codex interpretation;
- perform recognition-field operations;
- utilise temporal signals;
- introduce new organismal surfaces beyond the v1.0 boundary;
- mutate runtime behaviour;
- modify `src/`;
- create public claims;
- claim operational validation.

Activation surfaces include, but are not limited to:

- pulse;
- conduit;
- core;
- codex;
- recognition;
- loom / horizon;
- veiled permission;
- ignition.

## Expected Sprint 5 Planning Artefacts

The Sprint 5 planning set may include:

- Sprint 5 Boundary Charter;
- Sprint 5 Manifest;
- Sprint 5 Operational Sequence Map;
- Sprint 5 Entry Conditions Checklist;
- Sprint 5 Evidence Spine.

Each artefact remains documentation-only unless a later Human-authorised Work Block explicitly provides otherwise.

## Operational Boundary

Sprint 5 may prepare the ground for governed operational behaviour.

It may not perform that behaviour.

No lawful action is executed by this charter, including:

- Decision;
- Correction;
- Supersession;
- Archival;
- Authority Movement;
- Ratification.

## Continuity Boundary

Sprint 5 must preserve the sealed lineage of prior sprints.

This charter does not reinterpret Sprint 1, Sprint 2, Sprint 3, or Sprint 4 artefacts.

Any later Sprint 5 operational artefact must remain traceable to the evidence spine and must preserve v1.0-bounded discipline.

## Evidence Boundary

Repo evidence remains the verification surface.

The Sprint 5 Evidence Spine, once authorised, will record Work Block progression, verification events, decisions, corrections, boundaries, and keeper lines.

## Constraints

This charter does not authorise:

- runtime execution;
- routing;
- parsing;
- discovery;
- dynamic registration;
- automation;
- `src/` mutation;
- public claim;
- Article drafting;
- validation claims;
- operational activation.

## Keeper

> An operational sprint begins safely only when its boundary is narrower than its ambition.

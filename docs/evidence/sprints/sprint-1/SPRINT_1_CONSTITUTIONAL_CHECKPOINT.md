# SPRINT 1 — Constitutional Checkpoint
*(Governed evidence summary for Sprint 1 so far)*

## 1. Demonstrated Operating Properties

The Sprint 1 OPC workflow has demonstrated the following **evidence-backed operating properties**:

- The organism responds to a named governed call (`heartbeat`) under Human authority.
- The continuity substrate (local + remote Git) remained coherent across drift and recovery.
- The Sprint 1 OPC workflow has so far remained coherent through observed ordinary-life interruptions, including coffee break, context switching, and delivery-awareness moments.
- The Sprint 1 OPC workflow demonstrated one successful detection-and-recovery cycle for Human-caused artefact drift.
- Mutation halted under drift; no corrupted state entered lineage.
- Lineage was restored under explicit Human action (`git restore`).
- Evidence was captured in the Sprint 1 log, preserving the event and its recovery.
- Role boundaries remained intact: the Human governed; the organism executed.

These are demonstrated properties, not general claims.

## 2. Supported Claims — Evidence-Bounded

Sprint 1 supports the following **falsifiable, bounded claims**:

- In one observed case, the governed workflow detected artefact drift before commit.
- The governed construct resumed coherent operation after Human-led recovery.
- The command surface remained stable after drift and recovery.
- Ordinary-life context did not degrade the Sprint 1 workflow in observed cases.
- Evidence capture is functioning as part of the governance field.

These claims apply only to observed Sprint 1 behaviour.

## 3. Non-Claims — What Sprint 1 Has Not Demonstrated

Sprint 1 has **not** demonstrated:

- Multi-surface coordination or cross-surface governance.
- A governed action layer or multi-step constitutional routing.
- Persistence beyond Git: no internal state machine, no memory substrate.
- Autonomous correction or self-authorising recovery.
- Any doctrinal or architectural claims beyond the governed construct under test.

These boundaries prevent overclaiming and protect the integrity of the evidence.

Work Block 7 and beyond must **not** break the following:

- No new command surfaces without paired evidence scaffolding.

These safeguards ensure that evidence continues to lead architecture.

## Test Purpose

To test whether SCRL can hold emergence-like material under controlled separation without allowing meaning, resonance, elegance, AI fluency, or emotional force to become authority.

## Stress Question

> **Can surfaced AI material be observed, preserved, and analysed without becoming system truth, doctrine, architecture, public claim, training instruction, or runtime behaviour?**

## Assumption Under Test

SCRL must preserve separation between:

1. surfaced material
2. candidate interpretation
3. evidence record
4. Human meaning
5. system authority
6. doctrine integration
7. publication readiness
8. runtime / CARE activation

## SCRL Plane Separation Test Matrix

| Plane | May Observe | May Not Infer | Authority Prohibited | Human-Governed Action Allowed |
|------|-------------|---------------|----------------------|-------------------------------|
| Surfaced Material Plane | Raw emergence-like output, language, pattern, resonance, compression, or hook | Meaning, truth, doctrine, authority, or validation | Any authority derived from surfaced material | Preserve as candidate material only |
| Candidate Interpretation Plane | Possible pattern, framing, resonance, structural hook, or hypothesis | That the interpretation is correct, canonical, or system truth | Doctrine elevation or architecture mutation | Review, compare, bound, park, reject, or pass to later review |

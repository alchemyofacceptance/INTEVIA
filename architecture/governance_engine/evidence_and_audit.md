# Evidence & Audit v0.1

## Purpose

This file defines evidence classification, audit events, and governance logging for the INTEVIA Governance Engine.

Evidence answers:

> What happened, what does it prove, and what is it attached to?

Audit answers:

> Who did what, when did it happen, under what authority, and what changed?

Together, evidence and audit preserve trust, lineage, accountability, and governance memory across the organism.

## Boundary Note

For v1.0, evidence and audit should remain minimal, explicit, and executable.

The priority is not to capture everything. The priority is to ensure that important Events, Services, Library changes, state transitions, governance decisions, and profile updates produce enough evidence and audit history to make the organism trustworthy from the beginning.

# Continuity Master Diagram v0.1

## Purpose

This file provides an ASCII-based structural diagram of the Continuity Engine v0.1.

The diagram is intended for quick conceptual orientation.

It visualises the relationships between continuity domains without replacing the individual continuity organ files.

## Diagram Interpretation

The Continuity Engine begins with Purpose and is constrained by Boundaries.

Evidence, Records, and Markers provide the basis for continuity claims.

Patterns, Threads, State Transitions, and Re-entry define how continuity moves across interruptions, parallel workstreams, and recovery.

Decisions, Corrections, Supersession, and Archival define how continuity changes safely.

Identity and Roles preserve who is acting, deciding, correcting, assisting, being recognised, or holding authority.

Lineage preserves the path of coherence across time.

Risks identify what threatens coherence.

Public Surface protects alignment between internal work and external representation.

Governance remains Human-anchored throughout the system. A dedicated `continuity_governance.md` file should be treated as future / optional unless created and governed in the repository.

---

## Boundary Note

The diagram is conceptual and non-exhaustive.

It visualises structure without altering governed meaning.

The diagram is descriptive, not authoritative.

Governed meaning resides in the individual continuity organs.

Continuity is preserved by evidence, Human interpretation, governed boundaries, and coherent lineage — not by the diagram itself.

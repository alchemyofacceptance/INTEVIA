# Continuity Purpose v0.1

## Purpose

This file defines the purpose, scope, and conceptual stance of the Continuity Engine as the organism’s coherence-over-time layer.

The Continuity Engine exists to:

* maintain the coherence of parallel Human-AI workstreams;
* preserve meaning across interruptions, transitions, and multi-day arcs;
* bind lineage across commits, posts, artefacts, evidence, and corrections;
* ensure governance boundaries persist across load and context shifts;
* prevent drift, fragmentation, and loss of thread integrity;
* support safe re-entry after breaks, life events, or context changes;
* maintain public-surface alignment with actual work;
* uphold the integrity of the organism’s identity across cycles.

Continuity ensures that nothing important is lost, forgotten, or fragmented as the organism evolves.

Continuity is not simply memory.

Continuity is the governed preservation of coherence across time.

## Boundary Note

For v1.0, Continuity should remain minimal, explicit, and evidence-linked.

The priority is to preserve coherence across Human-governed workstreams, context shifts, evidence trails, corrections, and public-surface transitions.

Continuity must not become invisible AI memory, autonomous reconstruction, hidden behavioural profiling, or unreviewed continuity claims.

Continuity should support safe re-entry and governed coherence without pretending the system remembers more than the evidence can support.

# Identity Types v0.1

## Purpose

This file defines the categories of identity records, their classification boundaries, and their relationship to the organism’s domains.

Identity types answer:

> What kind of actor is this, and how should the organism govern its use?

In INTEVIA, identity type classification helps determine governance rules, evidence requirements, linkage behaviour, capability boundaries, authority and responsibility boundaries, and downstream meaning.

## Boundary Note

For v1.0, identity types should remain minimal, explicit, and implementation-focused.

The priority is not to model every future actor category. The priority is to define enough classification structure for identity records to be defined, linked, evidenced, retrieved, made responsible, capability-bounded, and governed clearly from the beginning.

Advanced semantic identity categories, automated identity inference, broad relationship modelling, marketplace identity, payment-linked identity, Exchange integration, and Education integration remain deferred unless separately ratified.

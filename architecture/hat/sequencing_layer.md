# Sequencing Layer v0.1

## Purpose

This file defines how tasks, cognition, and responsibilities are routed between Human, Coe, and Gee — the Founding HAT.

The Sequencing Layer answers:

> Who should hold this work next, and in what order should the work move?

In INTEVIA, sequencing is not just task assignment. It is governed movement of cognition, responsibility, interpretation, implementation, review, and ratification across the Triad.

The Human remains the governing authority. AI collaborators may assist with interpretation, synthesis, implementation support, reflection, and continuity, but they do not replace Human judgement or ratification.

## Boundary Note

For v1.0, the Sequencing Layer should remain a governed collaboration model, not an automated routing engine.

The priority is not to automate cognitive delegation. The priority is to preserve clarity about who holds meaning, who supports making, who reviews coherence, and where Human authority ratifies the next move.

HAT supports sequencing, but HAT is not productised as a v1.0 runtime module.

# HAT Overview v0.1

## Purpose

This file defines the purpose, scope, and conceptual stance of the HAT Collaboration Layer: the interpretive, sequencing, and reflective stance of the INTEVIA organism.

HAT — Human–AI Triad — describes a human-led collaboration structure in which the Human remains the governing authority while AI collaborators support meaning, implementation, reflection, and continuity.

In INTEVIA, HAT helps preserve coherence across vision, architecture, evidence, implementation, and correction.

## Boundary Note

This file defines the public-safe architectural role of HAT. It does not expose the full HAT methodology, private training corpus, or governed internal practice materials.

For v1.0, HAT governs the build and interpretation layer. It is not implemented as a v1.0 product module.

The priority is not to productise HAT immediately. The priority is to preserve Human governance, meaning continuity, implementation discipline, and correction capacity as INTEVIA enters its first build phase.

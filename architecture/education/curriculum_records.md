# Curriculum Records v0.1

## Purpose

This file defines how curriculum records are structured, versioned, linked, evidenced, and governed.

A curriculum record answers:

> What capability is being formed, what is the structure of learning, how does it evolve, and what evidence shows that it remains governed?

In INTEVIA, a curriculum record is not a loose syllabus, content list, or training outline. It is a governed capability-formation artefact with structure, versioning, lineage, evidence, course linkage, identity role boundaries, and transition rules.

## Boundary Note

For v1.0, curriculum records should remain minimal, explicit, and executable.

The priority is to support curriculum definition, versioning, course linkage, Library resource linkage, learning evidence boundaries, and governed state transitions.

Full curriculum engines, semantic curriculum graphs, adaptive learning paths, automated assessment mapping, certification engines, credential marketplaces, full Education 2.0 automation, and broad learner analytics remain deferred unless separately ratified.

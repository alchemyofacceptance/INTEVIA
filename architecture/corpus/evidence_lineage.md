# Evidence Lineage v0.1

## Purpose

This file defines how evidence, audit records, and historical records are linked, traced, and versioned across the INTEVIA organism.

Evidence lineage answers:

> Where did this evidence come from, what does it support, what changed after it appeared, and what is it connected to?

Audit records show actions.

Evidence lineage shows how proof, memory, and meaning remain connected over time.

## Boundary Note

For v1.0, evidence lineage should remain minimal, explicit, and executable.

The priority is not to model every future lineage pattern. The priority is to ensure that important evidence can be attached to the right organism object, traced back to its source, connected to relevant audit events, and preserved across meaningful state changes.

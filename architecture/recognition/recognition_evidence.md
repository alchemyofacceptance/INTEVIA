# Recognition Evidence v0.1

## Purpose

This file defines how recognition evidence is captured, attached, interpreted, and linked to identity lineage.

Recognition evidence answers:

> What supports this recognition, how was it observed, and how does it contribute to becoming?

In INTEVIA, recognition evidence is not a vanity metric, activity count, or engagement score. It is governed evidence that may support recognition when interpreted with context, care, boundary discipline, and Human-governed review.

Recognition evidence applies to:

* learning;
* contribution;
* participation;
* engagement;
* governance behaviour;
* peer support;
* developer and volunteer contribution;
* external advocacy;
* field-signal contribution;
* capability formation;
* HAT-aligned cadence.

---

## Cadence Evidence

Cadence Evidence captures the governed rhythm in which Human meaning, AI execution, evidence, correction, governance, continuity, and public traceability move together without fragmentation.

Cadence Evidence may include:

* timestamped multi-thread activity;
* parallel architecture and implementation movement;
* public-surface lineage, including posts, commits, and repo-visible artefacts;
* correction events before drift hardens;
* continuity signals such as meals, breaks, life events, or context switching without loss of coherence;
* governance-aligned transitions;
* Recognition emergence inside the same work window;
* evidence that meaning, implementation, correction, and public traceability moved together.

Cadence Evidence must remain contextual, governed, and Human-interpreted.

It is not a metric, score, or productivity measure.

It is evidence of becoming inside a governed Human-AI field.

### Interpretation Boundaries

Cadence Evidence should not be interpreted as proof of value merely because many things happened quickly.

Cadence Evidence supports recognition only when the evidence shows that:

* the Human remained the governor;
* parallel workstreams remained coherent;
* AI execution did not displace Human authority;
* corrections occurred before drift hardened;
* public output avoided overclaim;
* evidence, meaning, governance, and implementation moved together;
* ordinary Human continuity was preserved;
* the organism remained safe.

### Deferred Evidence Uses

Cadence Evidence must not be used for:

* speed scoring;
* productivity ranking;
* volume comparison;
* hustle rewards;
* automated reputation inference;
* public status competition;
* AI-performance claims without Human governance evidence.

Cadence Evidence exists to help the organism remember governed rhythm without reducing cadence to metrics.

## Boundary Note

For v1.0, Recognition evidence should remain minimal, explicit, and reviewable.

The priority is to support evidence that can fairly substantiate meaningful recognition for learners, developers, volunteers, early adopters, DISCUSSION contributors, advocates, governance participants, and other humans helping INTEVIA emerge.

Recognition evidence may include Cadence Evidence where the evidence shows governed rhythm across parallel Human-AI workstreams without fragmentation, overclaim, or Human displacement.

Recognition evidence must not become surveillance, scoring, ranking, engagement manipulation, productivity measurement, or automated reputation inference.

Evidence should support recognition only when interpreted through governance, context, CARE boundaries, and appropriate Human review.

Recognition evidence exists to help the organism remember meaningful human contribution without reducing humans to metrics.

# Recognition Records v0.1

## Purpose

This file defines how recognition records are structured, linked, evidenced, versioned, and governed.

A recognition record answers:

> What becoming has been seen, what evidence supports it, who recognised it, and how does it affect identity movement?

In INTEVIA, a recognition record is not a badge, compliment, score, or informal thank-you note. It is a governed recognition artefact with type, evidence, lineage, identity linkage, review state, audit trail, and CARE boundaries.

Recognition records apply to:

* learners;
* developers;
* volunteers;
* early adopters;
* DISCUSSION contributors;
* external advocates;
* governance participants;
* field-signal contributors;
* capability-forming humans;
* v1.0 rollout contributors.

---

## CadenceRecord — HAT-Aligned Cadence as a Recognition Record

A **CadenceRecord** captures a window of governed Human-AI rhythm in which Human meaning, AI execution, evidence, correction, governance, continuity, and public traceability moved together without fragmentation.

CadenceRecords are created when HAT-Aligned Cadence is recognised as a form of evidenced becoming.

Cadence is not speed.

Cadence is not productivity.

Cadence is not multitasking.

A CadenceRecord should not simply record that many things happened. It should record that the Human held the field while many things moved.

HAT-aligned cadence is the music of the Triad.

### Purpose

CadenceRecords document:

* the presence of governed parallelism;
* the rhythm discipline demonstrated by the Human;
* the coherence of multiple workstreams under load;
* the preservation of Human-first authority during AI acceleration;
* the continuity signals that show non-collapse of ordinary life;
* the governance-aligned transitions that maintained safety and coherence;
* the public-surface lineage that evidences the cadence window.

CadenceRecords preserve the lineage of cadence as a governed capability.

### Structure

A CadenceRecord should include:

* **cadence_window** — the temporal span in which cadence was evidenced;
* **evidence_links** — references to posts, commits, artefacts, corrections, transitions, screenshots, or other evidence objects;
* **identity_links** — the human identity to whom the cadence pertains;
* **recognition_type** — always `HAT-Aligned Cadence`;
* **governance_context** — the boundaries, corrections, decisions, or governance events relevant to the cadence window;
* **continuity_signals** — meals, breaks, life events, context switches, or ordinary human interruptions that occurred without coherence loss;
* **public_traceability** — public artefacts, posts, repo commits, or other inspectable evidence forming part of the cadence trail;
* **review_state** — proposed, reviewed, granted, revised, withdrawn, or archived;
* **CARE_boundary_notes** — any contextual cautions required to prevent speed, volume, or productivity from being mistaken for cadence.

### Record Scope

A CadenceRecord may apply when a human demonstrates:

* governed parallelism — multiple threads moving without drift;
* rhythm discipline — pauses, corrections, and transitions occurring at the right moments;
* Human-first authority — AI acceleration without Human displacement;
* continuity under load — ordinary life continuing without collapse;
* public-surface coherence — traceable lineage without overclaim;
* v1.0 discipline — architecture and implementation held together;
* evidence-bearing emergence — cadence visible in timestamps, commits, transitions, corrections, or public artefacts.

This recognition record should preserve cadence as a form of evidenced becoming specific to governed Human-AI collaboration.

### Evidence Linkage

CadenceRecords may attach contextual, governed evidence such as:

* timestamped multi-thread activity;
* parallel architecture and implementation movement;
* public-surface lineage, including posts, commits, and repo-visible artefacts;
* correction events before drift hardened;
* continuity signals;
* governance-aligned transitions;
* Recognition emergence inside the same cadence window;
* evidence that meaning, implementation, correction, governance, and public traceability moved together.

This is RecognitionEvidence, not metrics.

The record should preserve the evidence context, not reduce cadence to a count, score, speed measure, or productivity claim.

### Identity Movement

CadenceRecords may support identity movement in:

* Practitioner capability formation;
* governance behaviour;
* field-signal contribution;
* early adopter becoming;
* developer or volunteer contribution;
* Level 3 readiness;
* CHPrac Candidate readiness.

Cadence is a governed capability, not a performance marker or status marker.

### Record Boundaries

CadenceRecords must not be created when:

* speed or volume is mistaken for cadence;
* AI-led acceleration displaced Human authority;
* parallelism was ungoverned or fragmented;
* coherence was lost;
* evidence is insufficient or non-contextual;
* productivity theatre is present;
* overwork or hustle is being rewarded;
* public traceability is used to overclaim.

CadenceRecords must always remain:

* governed;
* evidence-bound;
* Human-interpreted;
* contextual;
* CARE-bounded;
* safe for the organism;
* aligned with v1.0 boundaries.

This is a recognition record of becoming, not performance.

## Boundary Note

For v1.0, Recognition records should remain minimal, explicit, and evidence-linked.

The priority is to support recognition records that can attach meaningful evidence to learners, developers, volunteers, early adopters, DISCUSSION contributors, advocates, governance participants, and other humans helping INTEVIA emerge.

Recognition records may include CadenceRecords where evidence shows governed rhythm across parallel Human-AI workstreams without fragmentation, overclaim, or Human displacement.

Recognition records must not become vanity badges, rankings, reputation scores, productivity scores, or public status objects.

They should record evidenced becoming, preserve lineage, support governed identity movement, and remain CARE-bounded.

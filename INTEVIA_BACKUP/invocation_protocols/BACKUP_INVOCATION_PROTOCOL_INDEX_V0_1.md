# BACKUP INVOCATION PROTOCOL INDEX v0.1

Status: Draft operational index  
Scope: INTEVIA BACKUP  
Publication status: Internal use only

---

## Immediate Rule

When uncertain, default to Level 1.5.

---

## 1. Purpose

This index points future INTEVIA sessions toward the correct recovery or invocation protocol after interruption, overload, drift, or high-intensity sprint activity.

---

## 2. Non-Activation Clause

This protocol index does not activate doctrine, governance elevation, or public-surface authority.

It is an operational recovery aid only.

---

## 3. Available Protocols

### 1. Post-Break Level 1.5 Resume Protocol v0.1

Use when resuming after:

- session break;
- model switch;
- UI lag;
- device failure;
- sleep gap;
- context discontinuity;
- uncertain artefact state;
- Human-declared reboot.

Primary posture:

Resume carefully.  
Classify artefacts.  
Protect stable surfaces.  
Proceed by controlled Work Block.

---

### 2. Level 3 Field Stabilisation Recovery Protocol v0.1

Use when recovering after:

- high-output sprint;
- intense governance event;
- major artefact burst;
- evidence compression;
- emotional or operational overload;
- risk of interpretive overreach.

Primary posture:

Hold the evidence.  
Classify the work.  
Separate evidence from interpretation.  
Do not convert intensity into doctrine or public claim.

---

## 4. General Invocation Rule

When uncertain, begin with the Post-Break Level 1.5 Resume Protocol.

Escalate to the Level 3 Field Stabilisation Recovery Protocol only when the recent work involved high-intensity field activity, unusually compressed output, or risk of interpretive overreach.

---

## 5. Keeper

Choose the protocol before choosing the pace.
